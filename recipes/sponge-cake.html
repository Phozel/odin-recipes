Swedish Sponge Cake

Description

The classic Swedish sponge cake. Soft, fluffy, delicious, and o-so easy to make.

Whether you're a beginner or experienced baker you will never go wrong with this cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 Long pans
  • 1-2L Soft butter and flour for the pans
  • 150g Butter
  • 2dl Milk
  • 1 Lemon
  • 250g Egg (approx. 5 eggs)
  • 500g Granulated Sugar (approx. 6dl)
  • 500g Flour (approx. 8dl)
  • 10g baking soda (approx. 2tsp)

Steps

  1. Warm the oven to 180C.
  2. Butter and cover the inside of the pans thoroughly, shake off any excess flour.
  3. Melt the the butter and add the milk before placing the mixture to the side.
  4. Wash the lemon and zest it.
  5. Mix eggs, sugar, and lemon zest with an electric mixer until the mixture is thick and fluffy.
  6. Pass flour and baking soda through a sieve and into the egg-mix, mixing them together.
  7. Mix the batter and mix it thoroughly with the melted butter and milk.
  8. Fill the pans about half way and put them in the lower part of the oven.
  9. Bake the cakes until they are golden-brown, should take about 35-40 minutes. Poke the cakes with a thin stick or knife to test if they're done, if the utensil is dry afterwards then the cake is done.
  10. Let the cakes cool down before eating.

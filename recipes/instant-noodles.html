Instant Noodles

Description

One of the easiest meals possible to make. Perfect for broke students.

Recipe made for one portion, adapt ingredients if more portions are needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 Glass bowl and plate that can cover it
  • 1 Square of instant noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Open the Square of instant noodles and place them in the bowl. You may crush the noodles to make it easier to eat with a spoon.
  2. Open any accompanying spice and oil packs and add them to the bowl.
  3. Boil approximately 1L of water (exact amount varies on bowl and noodle square size).
  4. Fill the bowl with boiling water and cover with a plate.
  5. Wait until no noodles are floating anymore, at which point they are ready to eat.
